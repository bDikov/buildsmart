# Product Guidelines: BuildSmart

## Prose Style: Technical & Objective
- **Tone**: Use clear, direct, and technical language suitable for professionals (contractors, admins, and quantity surveyors).
- **Voice**: Maintain an objective, professional voice that prioritizes clarity and precision. Avoid conversational filler or overly casual language.
- **Terminology**: Consistently use industry-standard construction terms and labels that align with the "Professional Grade" vision.

## Branding & Visuals: Minimalist & Modern
- **Minimalist & Functional**: Prioritize a clean, functional UI that emphasizes information hierarchy and task management over decorative elements.
- **Modern & Interactive**: Incorporate modern, interactive components like progress indicators, icons, and clear visual feedback for AI-powered features.
- **Color Palette**: Use a cohesive, professional color scheme that reflects trustworthiness and modern technology.

## UX Principles: Mobile-First Interaction
- **On-Site Optimization**: Optimize all key workflows (Job Wizard, Scope Review, Feedback) for mobile-first interactions, including large tap targets and swipe gestures.
- **Direct Navigation**: Ensure that homeowners and tradesmen can easily access their projects and tasks while on-site.
- **Accessibility**: Maintain high accessibility standards for all users across various device sizes.

## Documentation & Writing Style: Explanatory
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Provide detailed, explanatory documentation for all features, ensuring that users fully understand the "how" and "why" behind each tool.
- **Structured Content**: Use headings and clear sections to organize long-form content, making it easier to navigate while remaining thorough.
- **Consistency**: Ensure all technical guides and UI labels follow a unified naming convention.
