# Product Definition: BuildSmart

## Initial Concept
BuildSmart is a .NET 9 application following Clean Architecture, designed for homeowners to manage renovation projects with professional-grade construction management tools.

## Target Users
- **Homeowners**: Seeking a professional renovation experience with clear communication and visibility into their project's progress.

## Primary Goals
- **Communication**: Improve clarity between homeowners and admins via a threaded feedback system, ensuring all questions and clarifications are resolved before work begins.

## Key Features
- **AI Scope Generation**: Automatically transform raw Q&A into professional Markdown Scopes of Work, including project overviews, detailed tasks, materials, and site logistics.
- **Project Dashboard**: Manage entire renovation projects as a single entity, providing a "Full Picture" view of all related job tasks and metadata.
- **Clarification System**: Enable detailed, threaded Q&A on specific job tasks between admins and homeowners to resolve issues individually.

## Platform Vision
- **Mobile-First UX**: A seamless mobile experience for homeowners and tradesmen on-site, built with .NET MAUI.
- **Professional Grade**: A professional tool for quantity surveyors and construction managers to provide accurate and objective construction guidance.
